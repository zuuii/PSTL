Do you want to let the steam out after a long day of studying, while you are also working out in the same time? Do you wish to make new friends with the same interest as yours? Come and join one or more of our sport activities, organised each week!

Volleyball

Similar to the other activities on this page, volleyball is another team sport which enhances your capability to work together with your teammates while trying to score more points than the opposite team.

Thursday 5pm @ RGU
Basketball

If you enjoy shooting some hoops, while also be able to learn the rules of this game, alongside with some tips and tricks come and join this activity. Basketball is held on every second tuesday. For the next available session please check the calendar down below!

Tuesday 6pm @ RGU

Rugby

Rugby is a widely known full contact team sport. Rugby is one of the most popular participation and spectator sport in Scotland. So if you would like to learn more about this game or simply just play it with the rest of us, come and join us for the next available session!

Friday 5.30pm @ RGU
Soccer

Originated from England, and first played in the mid-19th century, soccer has become one of the most well-known sport in the world. For the next time we are playing soccer together, please check the event calendar!

Tuesday 5pm @ RGU
