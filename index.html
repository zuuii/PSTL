EXTENDING OUR SPORT ACTIVITIES!

We can surely be very excited about December and what this month is secretly holding for us. Due to the great interest that our members have shown towards basketball, we could leave unnoticed. Hence working together with the university, we were able to rent out one of the indoor areas at RGU Sport to organize basketball sessions, which will last for hours and held every second week. For more information about this activity please visit our “Activities” webpage.

PTLS Secret Santa

Since it is almost that time of the year again, when we draw each other`s names and become someone else`s secret Santa, we are happy to announce that we will of course continue the tradition this year as well. If you would wish to participate in the festivities, please get in touch with us and we will add your name to the list. Your gift is your choice, it can be big or small, but it will surely put smiles on your chosen one`s face.

LAUNCHING A NEW COMMUNITY EVENT FOR OUR MEMBERS! HOSTED BY ROBERT

December is just around the corner! This is the time of the year when days are getting shorter and nights are getting veryyyyyyy long. Also due to the Scottish weather it can be extremely difficult sometimes to go outside and meet up with your pals. Hence our society is launching our new, hopefully, weekly event, when we can meet up indoors and watch a couple of our favourites movies together. I really do hope that you are all excited that this is happening from next week. For more information and the date and the place, please check out our “community events” webpage
